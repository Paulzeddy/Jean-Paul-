About Me

I am an experienced and certified English–Kinyarwanda teacher, translator, and digital skills trainer. With a Bachelor’s Degree in English and Kinyarwanda, I’ve taught learners in multiple Rwandan schools and contributed to AI-based translation projects with GIZ, CLEAR Global, and Digital Umuganda.

My vision is to build smart learning solutions, like SmartLang, that make education more accessible in Rwanda and beyond. I blend education, creativity, and technology to help others succeed.
